Our Projects

MetaBooking

Booking process always begins with visual identification of hotel and the premises. Conventionally, travelers mainly have photos as their primary tool for exploring their options. MetaBooking is a 3D visualization solution provider that equips NOMADAO's partner hotels with tools and designs they need to create their digital space and allow clients to explore with full freedom.

NomadVerse

NomadVerse gives community members and guests of partner hotels to enjoy virtual experiences and tours, attend events & concerts, play games and meet with others without leaving their room. Hotels can offer additional services and facilities within the premises of their virtual architecture.

NomadAI

Nomad AI is a pricing and demand forcasting tool designed for hotels to appropriately price their rooms and optimize their ADR. It offers historic data analytics for hotels and their competitors, while providing tools for forecasts. The neural link also incorporates external factors that affect prices such as the weather, cultural events and global trends. The tool is accessible to the members of the community and is used by the community to manage NOMADAO's community fund.

Nomad Pay

NomadPay is an cryptocurrency exchange platform and payment solutions provider for hotels. It allows clients to seamlessly use hotel's facilities, while paying in desired cryptocurrency. NomadPay also supports a network of crypto terminals, kiosks and ATMs that all seamlessly work together with hotel's internal management tools.

NomadPerson

NomadPerson offers tokenization solutions for personal and corporate brands to create tradable digital assets of their identity. These assets, while aesthetically appealing, also carry utility and functionality specific to the brand. The community around the brand can then purchase a piece of their favorite persona or company, while getting various benefits and perks within the NOMADAO ecosystem, as well as outside of it.

CashLink

Cashlink is an all in one solution that allows community members to manage all their finances in one space. Ambassadors, affiliates, promoters, hotel management, travel agencies, tour operators, agents and travelers can all use this panel to manage their accounts, issue orders, collect payouts & cashbacks and participate in crowdfunding events.

about us

Travellers around the world unite to create and nurture a blockchain ecosystem designed and managed by its members, for the benefit every travel enthusiast across the globe. This ecosystem is a space to exchange exclusive goods and services, cultivate global relationships, share experiences and transact without boundaries or borders. Designed by experienced hospitality professionals and guided by executive management of global hotel chains, Nomadao was shaped to be a perfectly tuned platform for both the receiver and the provider of any service, information or item necessary to move and experience a free - Nomadic lifestyle.
